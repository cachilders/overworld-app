Sentiero

Fog-of-war exploration map for your workouts.

Sentiero visualizes your outdoor workout GPS data as a fog-of-war exploration map. Unexplored areas are fully occluded. As you walk, run, cycle, and hike, the fog lifts to reveal the terrain beneath.
